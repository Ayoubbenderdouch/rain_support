Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 9, 2024

Our Commitment: Rain Sounds does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Overview

Rain Sounds is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe that a relaxation app should be a safe space, free from data collection and tracking. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding your information.

Information We Do Not Collect

Rain Sounds does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Browsing history
  • Any form of tracking data

Offline Functionality

Rain Sounds works entirely offline. All sounds and videos are included within the app. No internet connection is required to use the app, and no data is transmitted to external servers during use.

No Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your usage of Rain Sounds remains completely private and is not shared with any external parties.

No Account Required

Rain Sounds does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app. Simply download and enjoy.

Data Storage

Any preferences you set within the app (such as timer settings) are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Rain Sounds is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy. The app contains no advertising, in-app purchases, or external links.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@rainsounds.app
