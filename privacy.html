Privacy Policy

Rain Sounds - Relaxation & Sleep
Last Updated: December 14, 2025

Your Privacy is 100% Protected

Rain Sounds does NOT collect, store, or share any personal data.
No analytics. No tracking. No advertisements. Just peaceful rain sounds.

Data Collection

Rain Sounds is designed with your privacy as the top priority. The app operates completely offline and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information or usage data.

What we DON'T collect:
  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Location data
  • Audio recordings or microphone access
  • Contact lists or photos
  • Any other user data

Offline Functionality

Rain Sounds works entirely offline. Once installed, you can enjoy all rain sounds without an internet connection. The app does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services.

Subscriptions & Payments

Rain Sounds offers optional in-app subscriptions to unlock premium features:

  • Monthly Subscription: $1.99/month
  • Yearly Subscription: $14.99/year

All subscription transactions are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple handles all payment processing, and their privacy policy applies to transaction data.

For information about how Apple handles your data, please review Apple's Privacy Policy.

Subscription Management

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings. Subscriptions automatically renew unless auto-renew is turned off at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

Third-Party Services

Rain Sounds does not integrate any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations

The only third-party interaction is with Apple's App Store for subscription processing, which is handled entirely by Apple.

Children's Privacy

Rain Sounds is safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any data, there are no privacy concerns for children using the app. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Since the app does not collect any data, changes to this policy will typically be minor and related to clarification or new features that continue to respect your privacy.

Your Rights

Since Rain Sounds does not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete privacy and control over your experience with the app.
